How to play Viisi

Viisi is a mindful daily puzzle. You receive five letters and must craft five different words using all of them. Every player worldwide solves the same board each day, so your streak is part of a shared ritual.

The basics

Simple rules keep Viisi approachable while still offering satisfying depth.

  1. Use every letter once per word.
  2. Words must be in the official Viisi dictionary—no abbreviations or names.
  3. Lock in each row of five words to progress to the next set.
  4. Leaving the game mid-row clears the partially typed words. Commit before you wander!

Helpful tools

Use these options if you feel stuck—your stats will note when hints are involved.

Hints

Find three words and the hint button lights up. Tap it to reveal two letters from your next solution.

Reveal

Ready to surrender? Reveal uncovers every answer in the current puzzle. Use sparingly—it resets your streak.

Reset

Need a fresh start without losing stats? Reset clears the current puzzle but keeps your progress history.
