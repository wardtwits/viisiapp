Privacy Policy for Viisi

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Viisi respects your privacy. Viisi is designed to be a simple, offline, privacy-first game experience. The app does not automatically collect, store, or transmit personal data during normal gameplay.

However, Viisi includes optional features (like sending feedback via email and sharing achievements) that involve data you choose to provide or share.

Information We Do Not Collect

Viisi does not automatically collect or process any of the following during normal gameplay:

  • Personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact details)
  • Device identifiers used for tracking
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or performance metrics
  • Advertising data or tracking profiles
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Payment or financial information

All core game logic runs locally on your device.

Optional Features That May Transmit Data

Feedback / Support Email (Optional)

If you choose to send feedback using the in-app email composer, your email will be sent using your configured email account. The email may include:

  • Your email address (as the sender)
  • The message content you type
  • Any attachments you choose to include

Viisi does not automatically read your email address or contacts. You are choosing to send the email

Sharing (Optional)

If you share a badge/achievement, the content you share (such as an image and a website link) is sent through the sharing method you select (Messages, Mail, etc.). Viisi does not control how the receiving service handles that data.

Third-Party Services

Viisi does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social login or sharing services
  • Cloud storage or remote databases
  • Crash reporting or diagnostics services

Optional features like email and sharing rely on Apple’s built-in system services and whatever external service you choose (e.g., your email provider or messaging app).

Children’s Privacy

Viisi does not automatically collect personal information and is suitable for users of all ages. If a child uses the optional email feedback feature, they may transmit personal information via email depending on what they write. Parents/guardians should supervise email usage where appropriate.

Data Security

Because Viisi does not run accounts or collect analytics, there is no central database of gameplay or personal data. If you email support, the security of that message is handled by your email provider and the developer’s email provider.

Changes to This Policy

If Viisi introduces new features that collect or process data (such as accounts, analytics, or cloud sync), this policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Viisi operates, you may contact the developer at:

Support@Viisi.app

By using Viisi, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
