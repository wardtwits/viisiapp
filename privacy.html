Privacy Policy for Viisi

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Viisi respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Viisi is designed to be a simple, offline, privacy-first game experience.

Information We Do Not Collect

Viisi does not collect or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact details)
  • Gameplay data or scores
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or performance metrics
  • Advertising data or tracking profiles
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Payment or financial information

No data is sent to or stored on external servers because the app does not transmit any user information.

Third-Party Services

Viisi does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social login or sharing services
  • Cloud storage or remote databases
  • Crash reporting or diagnostics services

All game logic runs locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Because Viisi does not collect any personal information, it is suitable for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since Viisi does not collect or transmit any data, no personal information is stored or at risk of unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Changes to This Policy

If Viisi ever introduces optional features that require the collection or processing of data, this privacy policy will be updated to describe those changes, and a new version will be published at this page before such changes take effect.
